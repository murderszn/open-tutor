# 🧮 4th Grade Weekly Math Quizzes

This document contains 12 weekly math quizzes designed to test standard 4th-grade math concepts throughout the curriculum. 

Parents: Have Elijah complete one quiz every Friday. Use the `assessment_grader.md` AI persona to grade these if you need assistance!

---

## Week 1: Addition & Subtraction (Multi-Digit)
1. Add: 4,562 + 3,891
2. Subtract: 8,204 - 5,637
3. Elijah has 1,250 baseball cards. Caleb gives him 375 more. How many cards does Elijah have now?

## Week 2: Multiplication Concepts
1. Multiply: 45 x 6
2. Multiply: 23 x 14
3. A bakery makes 8 trays of cookies. Each tray holds 24 cookies. How many cookies did they make in total?

## Week 3: Division Concepts
1. Divide: 84 ÷ 4
2. Divide: 135 ÷ 5
3. There are 56 students going on a field trip. If 8 students can fit in each van, how many vans are needed?

## Week 4: Place Value & Rounding
1. Write the number 45,672 in expanded form.
2. Round 8,452 to the nearest thousand.
3. Compare using <, >, or = : 34,502 ___ 34,250

## Week 5: Factors & Multiples
1. List all the factors of 24.
2. Is 36 a multiple of 4? Yes or No?
3. Is 17 a prime or composite number? Explain why.

## Week 6: Fraction Fundamentals
1. Draw a circle and shade in 3/4 of it.
2. Which fraction is larger: 1/3 or 1/5?
3. Convert the mixed number $2 \ 1/2$ into an improper fraction.

## Week 7: Adding & Subtracting Fractions
1. Add: $2/8 + 3/8$
2. Subtract: $5/6 - 1/6$
3. Elijah ate 2/5 of a pizza, and his dad ate 1/5. How much of the pizza did they eat altogether?

## Week 8: Decimals & Fractions
1. Write 4/10 as a decimal.
2. Write 0.75 as a fraction (bonus if you simplify it!).
3. Compare using <, >, or = : 0.4 ___ 0.04

## Week 9: Measurement (Length & Weight)
1. Convert: 3 feet = ___ inches.
2. Convert: 4 pounds = ___ ounces.
3. A string is 24 inches long. How long is it in feet?

## Week 10: Measurement (Time & Money)
1. If a movie starts at 2:15 PM and lasts for 1 hour and 30 minutes, what time does it end?
2. Add: $\$4.50 + \$2.75$
3. Elijah buys a toy for $\$3.25$ and pays with a $\$5.00$ bill. How much change should he get?

## Week 11: Geometry (Lines & Angles)
1. Draw two parallel lines.
2. What type of angle is exactly 90 degrees? (Acute, Right, or Obtuse)
3. Draw an obtuse angle.

## Week 12: Geometry (Shapes & Symmetry)
1. How many sides does a hexagon have?
2. Is a rectangle a type of quadrilateral? Yes or No?
3. Draw a square and draw one line of symmetry through it.

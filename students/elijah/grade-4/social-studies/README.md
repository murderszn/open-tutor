# 🌍 4th Grade Social Studies

Welcome to your Social Studies folder! Save your history notes, geography assignments, and projects here.

## Curriculum Goals (End of Year Expectations)

### 1. Geography
*   **The United States**: Know all 50 US states and their locations on a map.
*   **Global Geography**: 
    *   Name and locate all oceans and continents.
    *   Align major countries to their respective continents.
    *   Learn about the general language and religious profiles of different regions around the world.

### 2. Civics & Government
*   **Parts of the Government**: Understand the three branches of the US government (Executive, Legislative, Judicial) and what they basically do.
*   **Government Officials**: Be able to categorize government-paid officials into their correct part of the government (e.g., police, judges, president, senators).
*   **Local & State Leaders**: Know the names and roles of your local Mayor and state Governor.

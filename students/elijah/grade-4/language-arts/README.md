# 📚 4th Grade Language Arts

Welcome to your Language Arts folder! Save your reading notes, essays, and writing projects here.

## Daily Requirement
*   **Read for at least 30 minutes every day.**

## Curriculum Goals

### 1. Grammar & Mechanics
*   Master the **basic parts of a sentence** (Identify the subject and predicate, and recognize nouns, verbs, and adjectives).

### 2. Historical & Classical Literature
*(Note: These will be introduced through age-appropriate adapted stories, summaries, and discussions)*
*   **Ancient Epics & Texts**: Understand the high-level stories and lessons of:
    *   The Bible
    *   The Epic of Gilgamesh
    *   Homer's Epics (e.g., *The Odyssey*)
*   **Classical Philosophy & Drama**: Explore foundational stories and thinkers:
    *   Socrates (basic ideas of asking questions)
    *   William Shakespeare (Reading adapted tales from his famous plays)
*   **1500s-1800s Classics**: Read age-appropriate classical stories from this era.

### 3. Modern Literature Discussed (Post-Industrial Era)
*   Read and discuss **10 age-appropriate books** from the post-industrial era that hold significant cultural relevance. Discuss the characters, settings, and basic themes.

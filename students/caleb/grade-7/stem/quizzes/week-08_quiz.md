# 🧪 STEM Quiz — Week 08 (Lab Skills + Review)

New: Q1–Q6. Review: Q7–Q10.

Resources you may use (Learning Center):
- [Physical Science Roadmap](../../../../../assignments/science/physical-science-roadmap/README.md)
- [Lab Report Template](../../../../../assignments/science/physical-science-roadmap/lab-report-template.md)

1) Identify independent variable, dependent variable, and one control in a pendulum period experiment.
2) List two common sources of experimental error and how to reduce them.
3) Sketch a simple data table for measuring cooling water temperature every minute for 10 minutes.
4) You measure 1.20 m, 1.18 m, 1.19 m for the same board (true length 1.18 m). Comment on precision vs accuracy.
5) When is a line of best fit appropriate? What does R² indicate qualitatively?
6) What belongs in the conclusion of a lab report?
7) State Newton’s first law in your own words.
8) Give the relation v = fλ and solve: if v=300 m/s and f=150 Hz, find λ.
9) Name two heat transfer modes.
10) Balance (conceptually) the reaction: A + B → AB. What must be true about atom counts?

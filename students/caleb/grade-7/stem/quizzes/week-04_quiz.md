# 🧪 STEM Quiz — Week 04 (Energy & Heat + Review)

New: Q1–Q6. Review: Q7–Q10.

1) Distinguish temperature vs heat with an example.
2) Name the three heat transfer modes and give one real example of each.
3) Define specific heat capacity in words.
4) A 0.50 kg metal warms from 20°C to 40°C by absorbing 4200 J. Estimate its specific heat (assume no losses).
5) A 1.0 kg object at height h has potential energy mgh. If it falls to half the height, what happens to PE? Where does the rest go?
6) Explain energy conservation in a pendulum swing.
7) Ionic vs covalent bonding: one key difference.
8) Classify: melting, dissolving, burning as physical/chemical changes.
9) Convert 1.5 hours to seconds and to minutes.
10) Convert 32°C to K (Kelvin).

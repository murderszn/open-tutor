Connect the photosynthesis equation to energy flow and respiration.

What to do
- Write the balanced equation: 6CO₂ + 6H₂O → C₆H₁₂O₆ + 6O₂ (in words is fine if you can’t format).
- In 5–7 sentences, explain: role of chlorophyll, where it happens (chloroplasts), and how photosynthesis links to cellular respiration.

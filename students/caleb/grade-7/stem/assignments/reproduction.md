# 🧪 Reproduction — 7th Grade

Compare asexual vs sexual reproduction and mitosis vs meiosis.

- Quick guide: ../../../../../resources/biology_fundamentals.md#reproduction

What to do
- Write a short comparison (6–8 sentences) of mitosis vs meiosis: purpose, number of divisions, chromosome number, and whether the cells are identical or varied.
- Give one real example of asexual reproduction and one of sexual reproduction; explain a benefit of genetic variation.

# 🐾 Animal Cells — 7th Grade

Label animal cell organelles and contrast with plant cells.

- Quick guide: ../../../../../resources/biology_fundamentals.md#animal-cells

What to do
- Create a labeled diagram including: membrane, nucleus, cytoplasm, mitochondria, ribosomes, endoplasmic reticulum, Golgi apparatus, lysosomes.
- Write a short paragraph comparing animal vs plant cells (no cell wall/chloroplasts in animals; different energy capture).

Save as this file (replace these instructions with your work). Add a photo of your diagram if you prefer.

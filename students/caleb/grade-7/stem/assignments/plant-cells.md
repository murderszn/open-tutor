# 🌿 Plant Cells — 7th Grade

Label plant cell organelles and compare plant vs animal energy pathways.

- Quick guide: ../../../../../resources/biology_fundamentals.md#plant-cells

What to do
- Create a labeled diagram including: cell wall, membrane, nucleus, cytoplasm, chloroplasts, vacuole, mitochondria, ribosomes, ER, Golgi.
- Write a short paragraph comparing energy in plants vs animals (chloroplasts/photosynthesis vs mitochondria/respiration).

Save as this file (replace these instructions with your work). Add a photo of your diagram if you prefer.

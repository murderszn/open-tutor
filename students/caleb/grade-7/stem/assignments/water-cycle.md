# 💧 Water Cycle — 7th Grade

Model the extended water cycle and explain regional impacts.

- Interactive (core stages): ../../../../../assignments/stem/biology/resources/water-cycle/index.html
- Quick guide: ../../../../../resources/biology_fundamentals.md#water-cycle

What to do
- Create a labeled diagram or one‑page summary including: evaporation, transpiration, condensation, precipitation, runoff, infiltration/groundwater.
- Add a short paragraph (4–6 sentences) on how temperature, wind, and landscape affect the cycle in your region.
- Include one human‑impact example (e.g., urban runoff) and its effect.

Save as this file (replace these instructions with your work). Add a photo of your diagram if you prefer.

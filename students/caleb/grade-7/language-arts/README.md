# 📚 7th Grade Language Arts

Welcome to your Language Arts folder! Save your reading notes, essays, and writing projects here.

## Daily Requirement
*   **Read for at least 30 minutes every day.**

## Curriculum Goals

### 1. Grammar & Mechanics
*   Master the **basic parts of a sentence** (subject, predicate, nouns, verbs, adjectives, adverbs).
*   Progress into complex sentence structures, clauses, and phrases.

### 2. Historical & Classical Literature
*   **Ancient Epics & Texts**: Understand the high-level stories and cultural significance of:
    *   The Bible
    *   The Epic of Gilgamesh
    *   Homer's Epics (e.g., *The Odyssey*, *The Iliad*)
*   **Classical Philosophy & Drama**: Explore foundational thinkers and playwrights:
    *   Socrates (Ideas and methods)
    *   William Shakespeare (Reading selections from his tragedies, comedies, or histories)
*   **1500s-1800s Classics**: Read high-level, age-appropriate selections from this era to understand the evolution of literature.

### 3. Modern Literature Discussed (Post-Industrial Era)
*   Read and discuss **10 age-appropriate books** from the post-industrial era that hold significant cultural relevance. Topics will focus on historical context, themes, and societal impact.

**1.** What is a "chokepoint" or strategic fairway? A chokepoint (strategic fairway) is a narrow water route that is very important for global trade and shipping.

**6.** Where is the Strait of Hormuz located? The Strait of Hormuz is located between the Persian Gulf and the Arabian Sea

## 💼 Business Leaders & Entrepreneurs
*Focus on wealth creation, historic businesses, and modern moguls.*
* **Robert F. Smith** — Billionaire founder of Vista Equity Partners; leading philanthropist.
* **Madam C.J. Walker** — Historically recognized as the first female self-made millionaire in America.
* **Oprah Winfrey** — Billionaire media proprietor, talk show host, and television producer.
* **Daymond John** — Founder of FUBU, investor on *Shark Tank*, and motivational speaker.
* **Reginald F. Lewis** — First Black billionaire in the US context; built the massive Beatrice International.

---

## ⚖️ Government Officials & Civil Rights Leaders
*Focus on history, justice, and the political sphere.*
* **Barack Obama** — 44th President of the United States.
* **Kamala Harris** — 49th Vice President of the United States.
* **Thurgood Marshall** — First Black Supreme Court Justice and pioneering civil rights lawyer.
* **Shirley Chisholm** — First Black woman elected to the US Congress and first to run for a major party's presidential nomination.
* **John Lewis** — Civil rights leader, Freedom Rider, and longtime US Congressman.
* **Ketanji Brown Jackson** — First Black woman to serve on the US Supreme Court.

---

## 🔭 Science, Technology & Literature
*Focus on pioneers in STEM and thought leadership.*
* **Mae Jemison** — Engineer, physician, and former NASA astronaut (first Black woman to travel in space).
* **Neil deGrasse Tyson** — Renowned astrophysicist and science communicator.
* **Katherine Johnson** — Legendary NASA mathematician whose calculations were critical to early US crewed spaceflights.
* **Toni Morrison** — Nobel laureate and Pulitzer Prize-winning author (*Beloved*).
* **James Baldwin** — Novelist, playwright, essayist, and prominent voice of the civil rights movement.

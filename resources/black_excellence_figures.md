# Black Excellence: Study Them

A research guide highlighting historic and modern Black excellence across various industries and domains.

---

## 🎭 The Screen Icons & Entertainers
* [**Michael B. Jordan**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Michael_B._Jordan) — Historic 2026 Best Actor Oscar win for *Sinners*.
* [**Denzel Washington**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Denzel_Washington) — Continues to set the gold standard in prestige drama.
* [**Zendaya**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zendaya) — Starring in *Dune: Part Three* and *The Odyssey*.
* [**Viola Davis**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Viola_Davis) — EGOT winner and powerhouse of "must-watch" projects.
* [**Angela Bassett**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Angela_Bassett) — Redefining legendary talent and power on screen.
* [**Damson Idris**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Damson_Idris) — Transitioned to major film star with the blockbuster *F1*.
* [**Tessa Thompson**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tessa_Thompson) — Versatile lead in both indie films and the MCU.
* [**Mahershala Ali**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mahershala_Ali) — Two-time Oscar winner and intellectually respected lead.
* [**Colman Domingo**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Colman_Domingo) — Leading man status with a major career surge.
* [**Issa Rae**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Issa_Rae) — Influential producer and entrepreneur reshaping TV.
* [**Tyler Perry**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tyler_Perry) — Media Mogul & Studio Owner.
* [**Donald Glover**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Donald_Glover) — Polymath (Acting, Directing, Music).
* [**Quinta Brunson**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quinta_Brunson) — Television Creator & Star (*Abbott Elementary*).
* [**Keke Palmer**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Keke_Palmer) — Emmy-winning multi-hyphenate across all digital and film media.

## 💼 Business Leaders & Entrepreneurs
*Focus on wealth creation, historic businesses, and modern moguls.*
* [**Robert F. Smith**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robert_F._Smith_(investor)) — Billionaire founder of Vista Equity Partners; leading philanthropist.
* [**Madam C.J. Walker**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Madam_C._J._Walker) — Historically recognized as the first female self-made millionaire in America.
* [**Oprah Winfrey**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Oprah_Winfrey) — Billionaire media proprietor, talk show host, and television producer.
* [**Daymond John**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Daymond_John) — Founder of FUBU, investor on *Shark Tank*, and motivational speaker.
* [**Reginald F. Lewis**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Reginald_F._Lewis) — First Black billionaire in the US context; built the massive Beatrice International.

## ⚖️ Government Officials & Civil Rights Leaders
*Focus on history, justice, and the political sphere.*
* [**Barack Obama**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Barack_Obama) — 44th President of the United States.
* [**Kamala Harris**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kamala_Harris) — 49th Vice President of the United States.
* [**Thurgood Marshall**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thurgood_Marshall) — First Black Supreme Court Justice and pioneering civil rights lawyer.
* [**Shirley Chisholm**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shirley_Chisholm) — First Black woman elected to the US Congress and first to run for a major party's presidential nomination.
* [**John Lewis**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_Lewis_(civil_rights_leader)) — Civil rights leader, Freedom Rider, and longtime US Congressman.
* [**Ketanji Brown Jackson**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ketanji_Brown_Jackson) — First Black woman to serve on the US Supreme Court.

---

## 🔭 Science, Technology & Literature
*Focus on pioneers in STEM and thought leadership.*
* [**Mae Jemison**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mae_Jemison) — Engineer, physician, and former NASA astronaut (first Black woman to travel in space).
* [**Neil deGrasse Tyson**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Neil_deGrasse_Tyson) — Renowned astrophysicist and science communicator.
* [**Katherine Johnson**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Katherine_Johnson) — Legendary NASA mathematician whose calculations were critical to early US crewed spaceflights.
* [**Toni Morrison**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Toni_Morrison) — Nobel laureate and Pulitzer Prize-winning author (*Beloved*).
* [**James Baldwin**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/James_Baldwin) — Novelist, playwright, essayist, and prominent voice of the civil rights movement.

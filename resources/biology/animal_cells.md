# 🐾 Animal Cells — Quick Guide

Key organelles:
- Cell membrane: Flexible boundary controlling what enters/leaves.
- Nucleus: Holds DNA; controls cell activities.
- Cytoplasm: Jelly-like fluid where reactions happen.
- Mitochondria: Release energy from food (cellular respiration).
- Ribosomes: Build proteins.
- Endoplasmic reticulum (ER): Builds/processes molecules.
- Golgi apparatus: Packages and ships materials.
- Lysosomes: Break down wastes (common in animal cells).

Plants vs animals (quick): Animals have no cell wall or chloroplasts; shapes are usually rounder/irregular.

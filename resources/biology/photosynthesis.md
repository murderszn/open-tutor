# ☀️ Photosynthesis — Quick Guide

Idea in words: Plants use sunlight to combine water (H₂O) and carbon dioxide (CO₂) to make sugar (glucose) and release oxygen (O₂).

Balanced equation (7th grade):
6CO₂ + 6H₂O → C₆H₁₂O₆ + 6O₂

Where it happens: In chloroplasts (chlorophyll captures light).

Why it matters: Photosynthesis makes food for plants (and food chains) and releases the oxygen we breathe.

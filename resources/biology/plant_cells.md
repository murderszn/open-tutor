# 🌿 Plant Cells — Quick Guide

Key organelles:
- Cell wall: Rigid outer layer for support and shape.
- Cell membrane: Flexible boundary controlling what enters/leaves.
- Nucleus: Holds DNA; controls cell activities.
- Cytoplasm: Jelly-like fluid where reactions happen.
- Chloroplasts: Contain chlorophyll; capture sunlight for photosynthesis.
- Vacuole (large central): Stores water; helps keep plant firm (turgor).
- Mitochondria: Release energy from food (cellular respiration).
- Ribosomes, ER, Golgi: Build and ship proteins and other materials.

Plants vs animals (quick): Plants have cell walls and chloroplasts; animals do not. Both have mitochondria.

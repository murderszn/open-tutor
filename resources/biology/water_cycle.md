# 💧 Water Cycle — Quick Guide

Main stages and ideas:
- Evaporation: Liquid water warms and becomes water vapor (gas).
- Condensation: Water vapor cools and forms tiny droplets (clouds).
- Precipitation: Water falls to Earth (rain, snow, sleet, hail).
- Collection: Water gathers in oceans, lakes, rivers, soil.
- Transpiration: Plants release water vapor from leaves (bonus term).
- Runoff & Infiltration: Water flows over land or into the ground.

Key driver: The Sun provides energy for evaporation and movement.

Local examples: Puddles drying (evaporation), dew on grass (condensation), rainstorm (precipitation), river/lake (collection).

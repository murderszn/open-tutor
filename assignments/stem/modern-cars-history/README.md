# 🏎️ STEM Assignment: Automotive Basics

Welcome to your STEM assignment for the week! Please refer to the **[Automotive Basics Study Guide](../../../resources/automotive-basics.md)** to complete these tasks.

---

## 🧑‍🎓 Middle School Assignment

### Instructions
Create a new file called `my-engine-report.md` in your `grade-7/stem/` folder. Answer the following fully in your own words:

1. **Vocabulary Challenge:** Define these three terms in one sentence each: `chassis`, `axle`, and `transmission`.
2. **The Output:** What role does the `crankshaft` play in connection to the pistons?
3. **The 4-Stroke Cycle:** Describe the 4 steps of a combustion engine.
4. **Market Research:** Choose one of the major car markets (US, Japan, Europe, or South Korea) and write 3-4 sentences about two famous brands from that region.

---

## 👦 Elementary Assignment

### Instructions
Create a new file called `car-basics.md` in your `grade-4/stem/` folder. Or, you can draw this on paper and take a photo to save in your folder!

1. **Draw & Label:** Draw a car and label these three parts: the `chassis` (the bottom frame), the `axle` (connecting the wheels), and the `tires`. (If doing this on the computer, write a sentence explaining where each is located).
2. **The Engine Steps:** In your own words, list the 4 steps of a four-stroke engine (Intake, Compression, Power, Exhaust).
3. **Tire Detective:** Go look at a real car (Mom or Dad's car) and find the numbers on the tire. Write those numbers down and explain what the last number means!

---

**Submission:** When finished, commit your work to GitHub and show Mom or Dad!

# 🏎️ Assignment: The Perfect 3-Car Collection

Welcome to your first major coding and research project! 

## 🎯 The Objective
You are given a hypothetical budget of **$250,000**. Your goal is to research car brands, models, and their costs, and then build a website that allows a user to build their "Perfect 3-Car Collection" without going over budget.

## 🛠️ The Requirements

### 1. Research phase
Before you code, you need to understand cars! We have provided a resource to help:
*   **[Car Brands Research Portal](./car-brands.html)**: Use this page to find and check off car brands as you research them.
*   Understand the different models they offer and their average market prices.
*   You will be using an online repository of JSON data that hosts car brands, models, and prices to power your application.

### 2. The Application (Coding Phase)
You will build a website (using HTML, CSS, and basic JavaScript/Python as taught in your STEM curriculum) that does the following:
*   **Car Selection**: Allows a user to view and select different cars from the JSON repository.
*   **The Bucket (Garage)**: Allows the user to add up to exactly **3 cars** to their collection.
*   **Real-Time Math**: Calculates and displays the total cost of the cars in the bucket in **real-time** as they add or remove cars.
*   **Budget Constraint**: The total cost must be tracked against the **$250,000 budget**. If the user goes over budget, the website should alert them or prevent the addition of the car!

## 🚀 How to Start
1. Create a folder inside your personal workspace (e.g., `/students/<name>/projects/car-collection`) to hold your code.
2. Start by sketching out what you want your website to look like.
3. Write down a list of some of your favorite cars and see if they fit the budget!
4. Ask for help if you need a starter template for your website.

Good luck, and have fun building your dream garage!

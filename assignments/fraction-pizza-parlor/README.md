# 🍕 Fraction Pizza Parlor

**Grade Level:** 4th Grade (Elijah)
**Subject:** Math (Fractions & Decimals)

## 🎯 Objective / What You Will Learn
*   How to visualize and add fractions with like and unlike denominators.
*   How to calculate total costs using basic decimals and currency.
*   How to use multiplication to scale a recipe or order.

---

## 🛠️ Required Resources
*   Pencil and paper (or a digital drawing pad)
*   A calculator (only to check answers at the very end!)
*   *Optional but highly recommended:* Colored pencils to draw the pizzas.

---

## 🚀 The Project Details

Welcome to Elijah's Pizzeria! You are the owner and head chef of the most popular pizza place in town. Business is booming, but the cash register is broken! You have to calculate the orders by hand using fractions and decimals.

### Step 1: Design Your Menu
Create a physical or digital menu for your parlor. You must include:
1.  A store name and a logo drawing.
2.  Your "Base Cheese Pizza" price (e.g., $10.00).
3.  Prices for *at least* 4 different toppings (e.g., Pepperoni: $2.00, Mushrooms: $1.50).

### Step 2: The Fractional Orders
Four different customers walk into your store. Draw a circle for each pizza they order and divide it into slices. Color the slices based on what they want, and write the fraction next to it. 

**Customer 1:** "I want a pizza cut into 8 slices. I want 1/2 of the pizza to be Pepperoni, and 1/4 of the pizza to be Mushrooms. The rest should just be cheese."
*Tasks:* 
- How many slices are Pepperoni? 
- How many slices are Mushrooms? 
- What fraction of the pizza is just cheese?

**Customer 2:** "I am throwing a party! I need 3 whole pizzas. Each pizza is cut into 6 slices. My guests will eat 14 slices total."
*Tasks:*
- What fraction of *one* pizza did they eat? (Write as an improper fraction: 14/6).
- Can you convert that to a mixed number? (How many whole pizzas + leftover slices?)

### Step 3: Run the Register (Decimals)
Look back at the menu you designed in Step 1. 

**Customer 3:** "I would like to order one Base Cheese Pizza, add Pepperoni to the whole thing, and add Mushrooms to half of it."
*Tasks:* 
- Using your menu prices, add up the total cost of this pizza. (If a topping is on half the pizza, you only charge half the topping price!). 
- If Customer 3 hands you a $20 bill, how much change do you give them back?

---

## 📦 Deliverables
*   Your highly-designed Pizza Menu.
*   Your drawings and fraction math for Customer 1 and Customer 2.
*   Your total cost and change calculations for Customer 3.

---

## 📋 [For Parents] Evaluation Checklist
*   [ ] Did Elijah correctly slice the pizza into 8ths and identify that 1/2 = 4 slices and 1/4 = 2 slices?
*   [ ] Did Elijah correctly convert 14/6 into the mixed number 2 2/6 (or simplified to 2 1/3)?
*   [ ] Did Elijah successfully halve a topping price and add up the final decimal cost of the pizza?
*   [ ] Did he correctly calculate the change from a $20 bill?

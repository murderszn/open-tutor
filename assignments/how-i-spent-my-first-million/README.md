# 🤑 How I Spent My First Million

Congratulations! You have just acquired **$1,000,000** completely tax-free. However, there are rules to how you can handle this money. 

This is a budgeting and rationale exercise. You are required to spend every single dollar until exactly $0 remains. 

## 📜 The Rules

1.  **Allocate Every Dollar**: You must spend exactly $1,000,000. Your final remaining balance must be **$0**.
2.  **Provide Rationale**: For every single purchase (whether it's an investment, a donation, a business, a house, or a car), you must provide a detailed rationale. *Why did you buy this? What is its purpose? Is it an asset or a liability?*
3.  **Use the Tracker**: Use the included custom-built web application to track your spending and rationale. 

---

## 🚀 How to Start the Assignment

We have built a custom web application for you to track your spending!

1. Open the [**tracker.html**](./tracker.html) file by double-clicking it on your computer. This will open the application in your web browser.
2. The application will start you with a balance of $1,000,000.
3. Add your items, costs, and rationales step-by-step. The app will automatically calculate your remaining balance.
4. The application automatically saves your progress to your browser, so if you close the tab, your list will still be there when you re-open it!
5. When you hit exactly $0, use the "Print/Export as Document" button at the bottom of the list to save your final budget as a PDF to submit to Mom and Dad. 

Good luck, and spend wisely!

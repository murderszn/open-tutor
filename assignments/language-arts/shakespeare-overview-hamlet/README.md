# 🎭 Shakespeare Overview: Hamlet (Relevance Study)

**Grade Level:** 7th Grade (Caleb)
**Subject:** Language Arts (Drama & Culture)

## 🎯 Objective / What You Will Learn
* Understand the plot and central questions of Hamlet: revenge, doubt, conscience.
* Recognize archetypes: the ghostly messenger, the indecisive hero, corruption at the top.
* Connect Hamlet’s themes to modern leadership, ethics, and media stories.

---

## 🛠️ Viewing/Reading Options
Choose ONE:
1) Watch a short plot summary or teacher explainer video.
2) Read a plot/character overview with theme analysis.
3) Watch select scenes or a parent‑approved film adaptation.

---

## 🚀 The Project Steps
1. Plot Map (5 bullets): The ghost → The plan → The play‑within‑a‑play → Mistake → Fallout.
2. Archetype Spotting: Name at least 2 archetypes and where they appear.
3. Relevance Reflection: Use the standard template; discuss indecision vs. action and truth vs. manipulation in today’s world.

---

## 📦 Deliverables
* A one‑page plot map and a completed relevance reflection.
* 2–3 modern parallels (leadership dilemmas, misinformation, revenge arcs in media).

---

## 📋 [For Parents] Evaluation Checklist
* [ ] Plot map captures the key turning points.
* [ ] Archetypes identified and tied to scenes.
* [ ] Reflection includes concrete, modern examples.
* [ ] Writing quality: clear, structured, proofread.

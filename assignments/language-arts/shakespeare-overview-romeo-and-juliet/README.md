# 🎭 Shakespeare Overview: Romeo & Juliet (Relevance Study)

**Grade Level:** 7th Grade (Caleb)
**Subject:** Language Arts (Drama & Culture)

## 🎯 Objective / What You Will Learn
* Grasp the plot and core themes of Romeo & Juliet without reading the full text.
* Understand archetypes: star‑crossed lovers, family feud, impulsive decisions.
* Connect the play’s themes to modern movies, songs, and youth culture.

---

## 🛠️ Viewing/Reading Options
Choose ONE:
1) Watch a short plot summary or teacher explainer video.
2) Read a reputable plot overview with character/theme notes.
3) Watch a modernized film adaptation (parent‑approved).

---

## 🚀 The Project Steps
1. Plot Map (5 bullets): Setup → Rising conflict → Turning point → Tragic plan → Outcome.
2. Archetype Spotting: Identify at least 2 archetypes (e.g., forbidden love, feud, rash action).
3. Relevance Reflection: Use the standard template; explain how these themes show up today (e.g., peer pressure, online drama, loyalty vs. truth).

---

## 📦 Deliverables
* A one‑page plot map (bullets) and a completed relevance reflection.
* 2–3 modern references (movie, song, series) with 1–2 sentences each.

---

## 📋 [For Parents] Evaluation Checklist
* [ ] Plot map shows correct sequence and core beats.
* [ ] At least 2 archetypes named and explained.
* [ ] Reflection connects to specific, real modern examples.
* [ ] Writing is neat, organized, and proofread.

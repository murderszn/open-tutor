# 🔬 Physical Science Roadmap (Accelerated Grade Track • Foundational Grade Track)

Use this roadmap to cover core physical sciences with light chemistry, biology/anatomy, and physics. Pick one module per week; complete notes + one lab. Submit lab reports using the template.

---

## 📘 Accelerated Track (e.g., Grade 8-9)

### Module List (choose order or follow as written)
1) Measurement & Units
- SI base units, prefixes, significant figures, dimensional analysis.
- Use resources/weights_and_measures.md for quick conversions (US↔metric).

2) Matter & Atomic Structure (Early Chemistry)
- Atoms, ions, isotopes; protons/neutrons/electrons; periodic table groups/periods.
- Element vs compound vs mixture; physical vs chemical change.

3) Bonding & Reactions (Intro)
- Ionic vs covalent bonding; simple Lewis dots; balancing very simple equations.
- Reaction types (synthesis, decomposition) at a conceptual level.

4) Energy & Heat (Thermal Physics)
- Temperature vs heat; conduction, convection, radiation; specific heat concept.
- Energy conservation; potential vs kinetic energy basics.

5) Motion & Forces (Mechanics)
- Displacement, velocity, acceleration; graphs; vectors (magnitude + direction).
- Newton’s Laws; free‑body diagrams; friction; simple projectile intuition.

6) Waves & Light (Intro)
- Wave properties (λ, f, v); sound vs light; EM spectrum overview; reflection/refraction basics.

7) Biology & Anatomy (Survey)
- Cells (organelles and functions); tissues → organs → systems.
- Systems overview: circulatory, respiratory, digestive, nervous; homeostasis.

8) Lab Skills & Communication
- Variables, controls, trials; data tables; graphing; sources of error.
- Write clean lab reports; see template.

### Deliverables per Module
- 1–2 pages of notes in your own words.
- 1 lab from the Advanced Lab Menu with a completed lab report.

---

## 📗 Foundational Track (Catch‑Up)

### Module List (same topics at lighter depth)
1) Measurement & Units
- Metric system basics (meter, liter, gram) and common prefixes.
- Simple US↔metric conversions using resources/weights_and_measures.md.

2) Matter & Changes
- Solids/liquids/gases; melting/freezing/evaporation; mixtures vs pure substances.

3) Energy & Heat
- Potential vs kinetic; heat transfer idea: conduction vs convection vs radiation (examples).

4) Motion & Forces
- Speed vs time; friction; gravity as a pull; simple machines (inclined plane, lever).

5) Waves & Light (Everyday)
- Sound (vibrations), light (straight lines, reflections), color basics.

6) Biology & Anatomy (Basics)
- Cells (as tiny factories); simple organ system overview (bones/muscles, heart/lungs).

### Deliverables per Module
- Short notes (half page to one page) + labeled sketch if helpful.
- 1 lab from the Beginner Lab Menu with a simplified lab sheet.

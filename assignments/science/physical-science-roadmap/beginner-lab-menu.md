# 🔬 Beginner Lab Menu (Foundational Track)

Pick one per module. Keep measurements simple and clear.

1) Measurement & Units
- Meter stick scavenger: measure 5 objects in cm; convert one to meters and one to millimeters. Bonus: convert a distance on a map from km to miles using the cheat sheet.

2) Matter & Changes
- Melting race: equal ice cubes on metal vs wood vs fabric; time which melts fastest; explain using heat transfer ideas.

3) Energy & Heat
- Solar warm‑up: place cups of water in sun vs shade; feel temperature change and record with a basic thermometer if available.

4) Motion & Forces
- Ramp run: roll a toy car down a ramp; measure time for two distances; compute average speed (distance ÷ time). Try a different surface and compare.

5) Waves & Light
- Sound maker: stretch rubber bands of different thickness; pluck and compare pitch (high/low). Draw what vibrates.

6) Biology & Anatomy
- Lung model (balloon + bottle) demo or measure breaths per minute at rest vs after 1 minute of jumping jacks; discuss why rate changes.

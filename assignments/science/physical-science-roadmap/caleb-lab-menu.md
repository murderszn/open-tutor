# 🧪 Caleb Lab Menu (Grade 9 Track)

Pick one per module. Use the Lab Report Template.

1) Measurement & Units
- Dimensional analysis challenge: Convert 65 miles/hour to meters/second; validate by timing a rolling object over a measured distance.

2) Matter & Atomic Structure
- Density column: Layer water, oil, and syrup; measure mass/volume to compute densities; predict floating order and verify.

3) Bonding & Reactions (Intro)
- Safe reaction rate: Vinegar + baking soda at different temperatures; measure CO₂ gas production by balloon circumference over time.

4) Energy & Heat
- Specific heat demo (comparative): Equal‑mass metal vs water heated for the same time; compare temperature change (qualitative if no thermometer).

5) Motion & Forces
- Pendulum period vs length; graph T vs √L; discuss linearity. Or cart on ramp: measure distance vs time to estimate acceleration.

6) Waves & Light
- Laser/flashlight and slit/edge: observe diffraction/spread (qualitative). Or sound speed estimate via echo (distance/time).

7) Biology & Anatomy
- Heart rate vs activity: record resting, after light exercise, and recovery; plot over time and discuss homeostasis.

8) Lab Skills & Communication
- Graphing and error analysis exercise: re‑graph prior lab data with best‑fit line; compute percent error if a reference is available.

# 🧪 Lab Report Template

Use this structure for every lab. Keep it to 1–2 pages unless you need more.

1) Title & Module
- Title:
- Module (e.g., Motion & Forces):

2) Question & Hypothesis
- Question:
- Hypothesis (what do you expect and why?):

3) Materials & Safety
- Materials list:
- Safety notes (goggles, adult supervision, no open flames, etc.):

4) Procedure (numbered steps)
- Step 1 …
- Step 2 …

5) Data
- Tables, measurements (with units), sketches/photos if useful.

6) Calculations
- Show your math (units on every number).

7) Results & Conclusion
- What happened? Did results match your hypothesis? What did you learn?

8) Sources of Error & Next Time
- 1–2 things that could affect results and how you would improve the lab.
